Asked Questions

We Need Your Help

What are the guidelines for making food waste donations?

-Freshness and Quality: Ensure that the leftover food is still fresh, safe to eat, and maintains its quality.
-Safe Handling: Handle the food with care to prevent contamination during the donation process. Use clean and food-safe containers to pack the leftovers.
-Temperature Control: If the food requires refrigeration, keep it at a safe temperature during transport to maintain freshness.
-Proper Packaging: Pack the leftovers in sealed containers to prevent spillage and maintain hygiene, Clearly label the containers with the type of food and the date of preparation.
-Communication: Communicate with the receiving organization in advance to understand their policies and any specific requirements for food donations. -Allergen Information: Provide information about any common allergens present in the leftover food to ensure the safety of recipients. -Quantity Consideration: Donate a reasonable quantity of leftover food that the receiving organization can manage and distribute effectively. -Legal Considerations: Familiarize yourself with local regulations regarding food donations to ensure compliance with any legal requirements. -Timeliness: Coordinate with us to determine the most convenient time for the donation.

How can I contribute to food waste donation through TABAQ?

-Online Form Submission: Simply fill out the donation form on our website. Click on the "Donate" button to access the form and provide the necessary details.
-Contact Information: All our contact details are conveniently located on the Contact page of our website. Feel free to reach out to us via phone or email.
-Visit Our Physical Location: If you'd like to drop off the donation in person, visit our physical location. Please ensure you follow the guidelines provided for a smooth process.
