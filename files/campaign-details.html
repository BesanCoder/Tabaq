Don't Let Your Leftovers Go to Waste

Why Participate?

  • Environmental Impact: Reducing food waste contributes to a healthier planet.

  • Community Building: Join us in fostering a sense of community involvement and social responsibility.

  • Support Local Charities: Your contributions to Tabaq will provide essential food resources to those in need.

How You Can Contribute:

  • Donate Your Leftovers to Tabaq: Instead of letting surplus food go to waste, consider donating it to Tabaq, our local initiative committed to ensuring that no one goes hungry.

  • Get Involved: If you're interested in actively participating, we welcome volunteers to assist with the collection and distribution of donated food to Tabaq.

  • Spread the Word: Help us amplify our message by sharing this campaign with your network, friends, and family.


How to Participate:

Visit contact us Page for more information and to get involved. When donating your leftovers, please contact us directly to coordinate the donation process. We'll provide you with guidance on how to ensure your contributions reach those who need it most. Every small action counts, and together, we can make a significant difference.
